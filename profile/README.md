## 🤖 About Us 

**Welcome to Open-Source Robotics For Myanmar!** 🇲🇲
We are a community-driven initiative aimed at advancing robotics, embedded Linux development, and open-source culture in Myanmar. Whether you are hacking hardware drivers or developing advanced autonomous systems, this is a place where anyone can learn, share knowledge, and collaborate on real-world projects.

> 💡 *“Let’s build the future of Myanmar's robotics ecosystem together through open-source innovation.”*
